How it Works

So, VPNs are based on the idea of what is called tunnels, meaning a path or channel as I described earlier above. These tunnels are made between two network communication ends, allowing data sharing through point-to-point connections. When someone sets up a VPN, it covers their actual location, allowing them to access websites or content. For example, if I want to access certain content on Netflix that is not streaming in the United States, I can change my VPN to Germany where the content is available.
