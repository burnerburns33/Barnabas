How to Set Up a VPN

  1. Choose a VPN service provider that fulfills your needs. Ensure they have a sizable server network and strong encryption technology.
  2. Install the VPN application on your device of choice.
  3. Launch the application and input your login credentials.
  4. Select a server location. For better speed, select one that is closest to your location. You can choose servers from any country that the service gives access to.
  5. Click the connect button to start the connection after choosing your server.
